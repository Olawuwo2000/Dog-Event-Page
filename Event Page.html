Dog Fancy Event 2022

The 5th annual Dog Fancy Event is being held in Califonia on December 22nd. This is the fanciest dog event of the year, starring over 100 different dogs. This event was made to show America how great dogs really are. There will be treats at the walk in, and for the dog owners there will be a buffet in the lounge. We thank you for coming to our event site, and hope to see you on December 22nd. Thank you.


Featured Dogs
